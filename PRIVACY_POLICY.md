# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 25, 2025

**Upnext: TV Series Manager** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personal information directly on our own servers. The App functions primarily as a client for third-party services and uses on-device storage.

### 1.1 Trakt.tv Integration
To utilize the synchronization features of the App, you may choose to connect your **Trakt.tv** account.
*   **Authentication:** Authentication is handled directly by Trakt.tv via OAuth. We do not see or store your password. We store the authentication token securely on your device to maintain your session.
*   **Data Usage:** The App sends and retrieves data (such as your watchlist, history, and ratings) to/from Trakt.tv to provide the core functionality of the App.
*   Please refer to the [Trakt.tv Privacy Policy](https://trakt.tv/privacy) for more information on how they handle your data.

### 1.2 Analytics and Crash Reporting
We use third-party services to improve the stability and performance of the App.
*   **Google Firebase:** We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This helps us understand how the App is used and fix bugs. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
*   Please refer to the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information.

## 2. Permissions

The App requires specific permissions to function:
*   **Internet Access:** To fetch TV show metadata, images, and sync with Trakt.tv.
*   **Network State:** To detect if the device is connected to the internet to optimize data usage.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App retrieves TV show metadata and images from the following open sources:
*   **TVMaze:** [TVMaze Terms of Service](https://www.tvmaze.com/site/tos)
*   **TheTVDB:** [TheTVDB Privacy Policy](https://thetvdb.com/privacy)

## 4. Data Retention

*   **On-Device Data:** Data synced from Trakt.tv or cached from metadata providers is stored locally on your device. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.
*   **Trakt Data:** Data on Trakt.tv is managed by you via the Trakt website.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/akitikkx/upnext).
