# Upnext: TV Series Manager - Google Play Store Listing

## Short Description (Max 80 characters)
Track your favorite TV shows, get airing notifications, and find where to watch!

## Full Description
Have you ever been frustrated by long production breaks to the point where you forget when your favorite series is coming back? 

**Upnext is your ultimate TV Series Manager.** Designed for television lovers, Upnext effortlessly tracks your watch schedule, reminds you of upcoming episodes, and tells you exactly which streaming service has the shows you want to watch.

Say goodbye to the friction of managing your watchlists and manual searching!

### 🌟 HIGHLIGHTED FEATURES

**📺 Your Personalized Dashboard**
Stay perfectly up to date. Your dashboard gives you a clean, at-a-glance view of the shows airing yesterday, today, and tomorrow.

**🍿 Where to Watch (NEW!)**
Stop endlessly opening different streaming apps! Upnext instantly shows you where to stream, rent, or buy a show in your specific country—whether it's on Netflix, Hulu, Prime Video, MAX, Apple TV+, or Disney+.

**🔔 Custom Airing Notifications**
Never miss a premiere again. Opt-in to receive smart, non-intrusive push notifications on the exact days your favorite shows broadcast new episodes.

**🌍 Explore & Discover**
Looking for your next binge? Browse curated lists of the **Popular**, **Trending**, and **Most Anticipated** shows directly from the Trakt community.

**🎭 Rich Episode Details**
Dive deep into your shows before or after you watch. View high-quality backdrops, cast and guest star lineups, crew details, exact air dates, and live community ratings. 

**☁️ Seamless Trakt Integration**
Connect your completely free Trakt.tv account to seamlessly sync your favorites to the cloud. Adding a show to your favorites creates a personalized release calendar that travels with you across all your devices.
